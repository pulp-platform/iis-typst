## v1.0.0 — 2026-03-31

Initial release on [Typst Universe](https://typst.app/universe/package/ethz-iis-thesis).

### Added

- Thesis/semester project report template for Master, Bachelor, and semester projects
  at the Integrated Systems Laboratory, ETH Zurich.
- Front matter: acknowledgements, abstract, declaration of originality, list of
  acronyms, table of contents, list of figures and tables.
- Back matter: bibliography, appendices, assignment description, and full declaration
  of originality.
- Shared ETH Zurich + PULP group logo header.
- Acronym management via [`acrostiche`](https://typst.app/universe/package/acrostiche).
- [Typst Quick Guide](../shared/typst-guide.typ) included as the default appendix.
